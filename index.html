Before & After Montefiore Square

Comparing the existing conditions with the redesigned vision.

Before

The space previously lacked openness, flexibility, and a strong sense of community engagement. Limited seating and underutilized areas reduced its potential as a gathering space.

After

The redesigned plaza introduces an open lawn, terraced seating, improved circulation, and flexible space for events, creating a vibrant and welcoming public environment.
