About the Park

This community park in Harlem features an open lawn with terraced stone seating, a garden with perennial plants, and a paved plaza that can be used for art installations, markets, festivals, and performances.

Location & Character

Nestled at Broadway, Hamilton Place, and West 138th Street in Manhattan, Montefiore Square is a sloping triangle that blends green space with flexible public areas for neighborhood gatherings.

Neighborhood Association

The Montefiore Park Neighborhood Association assists NYC Parks in maintaining this unique space, coordinating gardening efforts and encouraging community involvement throughout the year.

2021 Park Redesign

The Association played an important role in advocating for the 2021 park redesign, helping shape a park that better supports art, markets, festivals, and performances.

Events & Activities

From seasonal plantings to cultural events, Montefiore Square serves as a flexible backdrop for performances, markets, and neighborhood celebrations.
